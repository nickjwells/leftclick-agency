FAQ

Common questions

What happens on a discovery call?

We'll discuss your current workflows, identify automation opportunities, and give you a custom ROI projection. It's a no-pressure conversation—you'll leave with actionable insights whether you work with us or not.

How quickly can you deliver?

Most projects are scoped and architected within 1-2 weeks, with full deployment typically taking 2-6 weeks depending on complexity. We work in sprints with weekly demos.

What tools do you work with?

We specialize in Make, n8n, Zapier, and custom integrations. We also work with OpenAI, Claude, and other AI APIs. If you use it, we can probably integrate with it.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support. We also offer ongoing retainer arrangements for companies that need continuous optimization and new automation development.

What industries do you work with?

We work primarily with B2B companies across SaaS, e-commerce, agencies, and professional services. If you have repetitive processes that need automation, we can help.

How is pricing structured?

We offer fixed-price projects after the scoping phase—no surprise invoices. Pricing depends on complexity, but we're transparent about costs before any work begins.
