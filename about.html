Our Story

We saw the future of AI before ChatGPT existed.

In 2020, while the world was still skeptical of AI, we were already building production systems with GPT-2 and GPT-3. That head start became our competitive advantage, and now we use it to transform B2B companies.

From early AI pioneers to your growth partner

LeftClick was born from a simple observation: most businesses were leaving money on the table by not leveraging AI and automation effectively. Having built one of Canada's largest content agencies using generative AI years before ChatGPT launched, we knew exactly how transformative these technologies could be.

Founded in Vancouver in 2023, we've since helped dozens of B2B companies implement AI systems that generate leads, streamline operations, and scale revenue. Our approach is different because we've been practitioners, not just consultants. We've built, tested, and optimized AI systems in our own businesses before ever recommending them to clients.

2020 Using GPT before ChatGPT

We were deploying GPT-2 and GPT-3 in production systems when most people hadn't heard of large language models. That three-year head start informs everything we build today.

Our Journey

Key milestones

From early experiments with GPT-2 to building AI systems for companies worldwide.

2020

Early GPT Adoption

While most businesses hadn't heard of large language models, Nick and Noah began experimenting with GPT-2 and GPT-3 for content production at scale.

2021

1SecondCopy Launch

Leveraging GPT-3 for AI-assisted content writing, Nick scaled 1SecondCopy to nearly $100K/month, proving the commercial viability of AI in business workflows.

2023

LeftClick Founded

With ChatGPT bringing AI mainstream, Nick and Noah launched LeftClick to help B2B companies implement production-ready AI systems. Reached $72K/month within the first year.

2024

Community & Scale

Launched Maker School on Skool, growing to 400+ members. YouTube channel gained significant traction teaching Make.com automation. Combined ventures exceeded $100K/month profit.

2025

Enterprise Expansion

Expanding beyond SMB to serve mid-market and enterprise clients, with templated AI solutions and custom development for complex automation challenges.

Our principles

The values that guide every system we build and every client we serve.

Practitioner First

We build and test every system in our own businesses before recommending it to clients. Theory is useless without battle-tested execution.

We deploy fast and iterate faster. Most projects go live within weeks, not months. Your ROI starts the moment systems are running.

We don't just build and disappear. Every project includes training so your team understands, maintains, and extends the systems we create.
